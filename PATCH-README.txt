ACADEMIC GLOBE PATCH v11

This patch consolidates the requested background behavior.

What it changes:
- restores the more detailed center globe with clearer continent lines and global connection arcs
- keeps the flat world map only in the top header / main-nav band instead of across the full page
- keeps parallels / meridians in the top map so the map reads more clearly
- removes the red dot markers entirely
- keeps the globe fixed in the center of the screen while scrolling
- globe rotation responds to scroll speed and direction, scrolling down accelerates forward rotation and scrolling up reverses it
- moves the three school drawings from the footer area to small satellite-like markers orbiting around the globe
- makes publication / presentation / dissertation accordion items transparent while giving them a very soft hover / open outline and shadow so they do not disappear visually
- keeps the main nav and research / teaching sticky nav areas translucent and integrated with the site rather than solid white cards
