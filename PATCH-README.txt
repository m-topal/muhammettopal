ACADEMIC GLOBE PATCH v12

This version restores the real Globe.GL globe from v7, not the simplified SVG globe.

- restores the v7 detailed globe with country polygons and real world outlines

- adds a broader global connection network including Moscow, Tbilisi, Baku, Beijing, Delhi, Cairo, Africa, Latin America, East Asia and Australia

- keeps the globe fixed near the center of the screen while scrolling
- keeps the top flat map only in the header/main-nav band with parallels and meridians
- keeps school drawings as small satellites orbiting the globe
- removes red point markers
- makes main nav and section-tab names slightly bolder
- keeps accordions transparent, with a subtle hover/open outline and shadow only
