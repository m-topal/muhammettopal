<h1>Teaching</h1>

<p class="lead">My teaching introduces students to the modern Middle East, Islamic political thought, empire, nationalism, minority politics, and the historical making of political categories.</p>

<p>I design courses that combine close reading, historical contextualization, and discussion. My aim is to help students understand political and religious concepts as historically situated forms of argument, practice, and belonging rather than as fixed abstractions.</p>
