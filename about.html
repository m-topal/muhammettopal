About Me

I am a historian of late Ottoman intellectual life, with interests in biography, public intellectuals, the Caucasus, Istanbul, print culture, journalism, and the social worlds of intellectual production.

This website gathers my academic portfolio, research materials, teaching work, publications, presentations, and a personal blog.

The site is designed as a flexible academic home: formal enough for a portfolio, but open enough for essays, notes, videos, podcasts, and fragments.
